a few words about us

Shree Gopaldas Vallabhdas Jewellers: A Legacy of Trust and Excellence Since 1938

Shree Gopaldas Vallabhdas Jewellers was founded in 1938 by the visionary Gopaldas Ji Shroff. Initially, the business focused on trading raw gold and silver, supplying to local traders after sourcing from Bombay. In 1968, his elder son, Shri Damodardas Ji Shroff, joined hands to support and expand the business. By 1985, we ventured into retail, offering our customers the finest quality jewellery, which has become a hallmark of our tradition.

We take pride in being Burhanpur's first jewellery showroom and the primary supplier to 80% of the local traders. Our journey has been built on a foundation of trust, quality, and uncompromising standards, as evidenced by our BIS Certified Hallmarked products.

Following the passing of Shri Gopaldas Vallabhdas Ji on June 22, 1997, Shri Damodardas Ji took the helm, ensuring the continuation of our legacy. Today, his sons, Rakesh Shroff and Hitesh Shroff, work under his guidance, maintaining our commitment to excellence.

Our reputation extends beyond Burhanpur, with customers from Jalgaon, Indore, and across India praising the superior quality of our products. We have earned respect and trust, never compromising on quality, and adhering to government regulations.

As we celebrate 86 years of service, we remain at the forefront of technological advancements in the jewellery industry. Our dedication to accuracy and transparency is exemplified by our long-serving accountant, who has been with us since the time of Gopaldas Ji.

Under the meticulous supervision of Shri Damodardas Ji, we continue to uphold the values and traditions that have defined Shree Gopaldas Vallabhdas Jewellers for generations. Our loyal customers, some of whom have been with us since the days of Gopaldas Ji, and others who trust in Shri Damodardas Ji’s name, are a testament to our enduring commitment to quality and excellence.

Visit us to experience a legacy of unparalleled craftsmanship and trust.

Our Goals Shree Gopaldas Vallabhdas Jewellers' Goals

Nationwide Quality Reach: Our primary goal is to extend the reach of our high-quality products across India through multiple branches. We aim to ensure that our exquisite jewellery collection is accessible to customers nationwide.

Brand Recognition: We strive to establish a brand reputation that immediately conveys trust and superior quality to our customers. Our goal is for our brand name to be synonymous with excellence and craftsmanship, ensuring that whenever someone hears our name, they think of unparalleled quality.

Educating Customers on Quality: We aim to educate our customers about the difference between lower quality and higher quality gold. By doing so, we want to empower them to make informed decisions and recognize the superior value of our products.

Meeting Customer Needs: Our goal is to understand and fulfill the unique needs of each customer, providing them with the best possible products. We are dedicated to offering personalized and royal service that exceeds customer expectations.

Innovation and Tradition: We seek to combine the latest technologies with traditional craftsmanship to create unique and exceptional jewellery pieces. This ensures our customers always receive the newest and finest designs.

Expanding Access: We aim to expand our branches across the country, making it easy for every customer to access our premium products and services.

At Shree Gopaldas Vallabhdas Jewellers, we are committed to realizing these goals and consistently providing our customers with the highest quality and exceptional service.

Our Values Values at Shree Gopaldas Vallabhdas Jewellers

Uncompromising Quality: We are dedicated to providing only the highest quality jewellery. From sourcing raw materials to the final product, we ensure every piece meets our rigorous standards of excellence.

Customer Trust and Satisfaction: Building and maintaining trust with our customers is at the heart of our business. We strive to exceed expectations by understanding and fulfilling the unique needs of each customer, ensuring their complete satisfaction.

Integrity and Transparency: We conduct our business with the utmost integrity and transparency. Our adherence to BIS Certification and hallmarking standards reflects our commitment to honesty and regulatory compliance.

Legacy and Tradition: With over 85 years of heritage, we value the traditions that have shaped our identity. We honor the vision of our founders and uphold the principles of trust and excellence that have been passed down through generations.

Innovation in Craftsmanship: While we respect our traditions, we also embrace innovation. We integrate the latest technologies with traditional craftsmanship to create unique, timeless pieces that reflect both heritage and modernity.

Education and Empowerment: We believe in educating our customers about the nuances of jewellery quality. By empowering them with knowledge, we help them make informed decisions and appreciate the true value of their purchases.

Community and Relationships: We cherish our relationships with customers, employees, and the community. Our long-standing reputation is built on mutual respect, loyalty, and a commitment to giving back to the society that supports us.

Excellence in Service: Providing royal and personalized service is a cornerstone of our business. We aim to create a memorable and delightful shopping experience for every customer who walks through our doors.

At Shree Gopaldas Vallabhdas Jewellers, these values guide us in every aspect of our work, ensuring that we continue to uphold our legacy of trust, quality, and exceptional service.
